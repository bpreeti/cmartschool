Creativity is a state of mind. We all have great ideas, we just have to learn to express them. Learn the skills, the rest will follow..

Colorful Moments Art School offers art classes for kids and adults. Our class demonstrates that anyone can learn to draw and paint. Students will learn the fundamentals of drawing, painting, and design. Students will learn the correct techniques to drawing shapes, figures, and many other different objects. Once students are proficient in their drawing skills, they progress into the exploration of painting mediums. Students receive extensive instruction in the use of each painting medium, including color-mixing, painting techniques, and the proper use and care of materials. They will also learn how to express their imagination and feelings with various materials..
